HDPE (Pnhai) Pipes

Durable, flexible piping solutions for modern infrastructure

HDPE pipes and fittings offer numerous advantages, including non-toxicity, corrosion resistance, and excellent flexibility. Free from heavy metals, they are sanitary and ensure no secondary pollution. The smooth interior walls promote high flow capacity, while their flexibility allows for coil delivery, reducing installation costs.

Key Features

  • Non-toxic and corrosion resistant
  • Excellent flexibility for easy installation
  • Smooth interior walls for high flow capacity
  • UV-stable and impact-resistant
  • Lightweight for easy handling
  • Service life exceeding 50 years
  • Free from heavy metals
  • Reduces installation costs

Applications

  • Municipal and residential water supply
  • Industrial liquid transportation
  • Sewage treatment systems
  • Irrigation and agricultural uses
  • Mining and slurry transportation
  • Chemical processing
  • Gas distribution
  • Telecommunications conduit
