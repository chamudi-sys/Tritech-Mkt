Aquatherm polypropylene pipe systems

Aquatherm's journey began in 1973, founded by Gerhard Rosenberg to produce warm water underfloor heating systems. Starting in a garage and basement, the company has grown to become a global leader in polypropylene pipe systems for plant engineering and building services.

Aquatherm Blue Pipe

Designed for efficient heating and cooling distribution in closed systems

Aquatherm Blue Pipe is designed for efficient heating and cooling distribution in closed systems, as well as a range of industrial applications. Developed to prevent corrosion in heating systems, it quickly expanded due to its numerous benefits in various piping installations. Trusted worldwide, Aquatherm Blue Pipe is used in hotels, stadiums, schools, offices, and industrial projects.

Applications

  • Heating system construction
  • Chilled water technology
  • Chemical transport
  • Rainwater and irrigation
  • Compressed air
  • Floor-heating system
  • Ship building application
  • Geothermal
