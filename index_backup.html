About Us

Experts in industrial pump and pipe solutions engineered for efficiency, built to last.


Tritech Marketing International (Pvt) Ltd is a leading industry solution provider in Sri Lanka and a member of the Tritech Group of Companies.

We are the exclusive dealer for Speroni water pumps from Italy and also supply trusted brands such as Aquatherm, Heng Long, Evergush, Calpeda, Doori, and Puhui.

Our product range includes water pumps, PPR pipes, and GRP tanks, catering to industrial and MEP applications. With a strong commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, Tritech is your trusted partner for complete industrial solutions.
